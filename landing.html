Who should attend the conference?

The conference is ideal for entrepreneurs, business owners, executives, and anyone who is looking to grow their business.

What is the dress code for the conference?

The dress code for the conference is business casual.

Can I get a refund if I am unable to attend the conference?

Refunds are available for cancellations made up to 30 days before the conference date. After that, no refunds will be given.

Will there be networking opportunities at the conference?

Yes, there will be several opportunities for attendees to network with industry experts and other attendees throughout the day.

Are there any discounts available for group registrations?

Yes, discounts are available for groups of 5 or more. Please contact us at info@athen.com for more information.

Will there be any materials or resources provided to attendees?

Yes, all attendees will receive a conference program and access to any presentation materials made available by the speakers.
